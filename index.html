Workout guide
⚡
Strides
What they areShort controlled accelerations done after your easy run, not during it.
How to run themBuild to ~85–90% max speed over 5 sec, hold for 10 sec, ease off over 5 sec. Smooth, not sprinting.
Recovery60–90 sec walk or very easy jog between each rep.
Treadmill tipStart pressing the speed up a few seconds early. 5.8–6.2 mph (9:40–10:20 /mi) for the stride portion.
Why they workWake up fast-twitch muscle fibers and reinforce good form with almost no fatigue cost.
↗
Hill repeats
Structure8 min warm-up → 4–6 reps uphill → 5 min cool-down.
How to run themStrong, controlled effort at ~80–85% max. Each rep lasts 45–60 sec. Eyes up, slight forward lean from ankles, drive your arms.
RecoveryDrop incline to 0% and walk 90 sec between reps. Don't rush it.
Treadmill settings4.8–5.0 mph
